About

Engineering with systems-thinking energy

I'm a software engineer focused on tools engineering: internal platforms, developer tooling, workflow automation, and practical systems that make teams faster without making their work harder. My background spans games, creative tech, and VFX, but the through-line is consistent: build software that removes friction and holds up under real production use.

I care about product-minded engineering as much as technical correctness. That means clean APIs, reliable automation, useful interfaces, and systems that are maintainable by the people who depend on them. The goal is simple: make developers' and creators' lives easier with tools that feel solid, precise, and worth adopting.

It covers capture automation, developer workflows, documentation systems, and CI/CD — built from scratch to operate like a real internal platform.

It matters as a case study because it shows how I approach tooling as a product surface: structured systems with clean APIs, operational repeatability, and the developer experience of a real internal platform — not just a pile of scripts.
